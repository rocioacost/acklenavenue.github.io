COMPLEX INSIGHTS MADE SIMPLE

Our Partner

Our Partner is a Fortune Global 500 Company with a 150 year history and well over 100,000 employees. Like many legacy companies, our Partner is pursuing digital transformation on every level, and we are honored to participate in pursuit of their future.

Our Engagement

Before our engagement, our Partner was doing complex, mission-critical project tracking and forecasting work across multiple antiquated applications, and storing data locally across thousands of employee machines. There was no centralized repository to capture incredibly rich intelligence on project data. For a company of this scale, such insight has tremendous and transformative business value. There was also considerable opportunity for automation and increased efficiency, in addition to error reduction. Our primary goal was to provide a unified and intuitive forecasting system company-wide which would capture and make accessible valuable data on projects, while increasing accuracy and efficiency, and delighting its users.

Our Approach

Acklen Avenue dedicated one of our fully-assembled agile teams to this work, and kicked off with an on-site discovery. After creating user personas, we focused on a value-centric delivery approach, identifying the shortest route to solutions to real users’ needs.

It was also important to us to have a relationship with actual end users, and hear from them about their daily functions to build them the best possible project.

Over the course of the engagement, our team has developed a close collaborative relationship with the client, feeling far more like colleagues than vendor / client. The team even had a one-year anniversary party to celebrate the relationship!

Users can manage forecast more efficiently and gather new insights through new BI technologies.

Our Results

In less than 13 months, Acklen Avenue developed a complete project management application capable of replacing a thirty-six year old project management system. Thanks to our work together, our Partner now has access to the macro-level overview of their project management initiatives, and its users are more effective than ever as it is rolled out region by region.

Our partnership continues with new feature development.

Fun Fact

Although the system Acklen Avenue developed boasts over 50,000 lines of code, only 19 bugs have arisen in the entire life of the engagement, a testament to our core values of quality and integrity.
